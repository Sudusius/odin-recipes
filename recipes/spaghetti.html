Spaghetti

Image Credit

Description

A very simple spaghetti recipe for when you're feeling lazy. Spaghetti cooked in salt and then served with butter

Ingredients

  • 1/2 tablespoon butter
  • 2 ounces of dry spaghetti noodles
  • 1 cup of water
  • pinch of salt

Steps

  1. boil 1 cup of water on high heat
  2. add a pinch of salt
  3. add spaghetti
  4. lower temperature medium heat
  5. cook until the spaghetti is at the consistency you prefer
  6. drain water from pasta
  7. serve and add a 1/2 tablespoon of butter
