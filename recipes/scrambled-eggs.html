Scrambled Eggs

Image Credit

Description

Scrambled eggs are an easy meal to make and great for breakfast. This low calorie meal can help you begin your day with a light meal.

Ingredients

  • 1/2 tablespoon butter
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 tablespoon of milk

Steps

  1. start pre-heating pan on stove at medium heat
  2. break open 2 eggs into bowl
  3. add 1 tablespoon of milk into the bowl
  4. whisk eggs and milk in the bowl
  5. add 1/2 tablespoon of butter to pan and spread
  6. add egg mix into bowl
  7. scramble eggs as they solidify
  8. when eggs are no longer water and are solid, serve
